# Google Play Store Questionnaire Answers

## Category Classification

### Social or Communication
**Select:** **SOCIAL**

**Reasoning:** NexusMatch is used to connect people with large gaming communities, meet new teammates, share gaming content, and build squads. This aligns with the "Social" category definition (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram examples) rather than one-on-one communication like Skype.

---

## Content & Safety Questions

### Is the app used for dating or sexual relationships?
**Answer:** ❌ **NO**

The app is strictly for gaming matchmaking and team formation. No dating or relationship features.

---

### Does the app permit public sharing of nudity?
**Answer:** ❌ **NO**

This is a gaming platform. Users can share profile photos/avatars, but no explicit nudity features exist.

---

### Does the app permit public sharing of graphic violence (outside newsworthy context)?
**Answer:** ❌ **NO**

While users discuss games that may contain violence (e.g., shooting games), the app itself doesn't permit sharing graphic violence content.

---

### Does the app share user's current, precise physical location with other users?
**Answer:** ❌ **NO**

NexusMatch does not access or share GPS/precise location. Users can optionally provide general region/timezone information for matchmaking, but not precise coordinates.

---

### Does the app allow users to purchase digital goods?
**Answer:** ❌ **NO**

NexusMatch is completely free. No in-app purchases, premium features, or digital goods for sale.

---

## Community Features

### Does the app include the ability to block users or user-generated content?
**Answer:** ✅ **YES**

Users can block other players to prevent interactions and communication.

---

### Does the app include the ability to report users or user-generated content?
**Answer:** ✅ **YES**

Users can report inappropriate behavior, toxic players, or violations for community safety.

---

### Does the app include chat moderation?
**Answer:** ✅ **YES**

The app includes moderation systems for in-app messages, voice chat reports, and user behavior monitoring.

---

### Can interactions in the app be limited to invited friends only?
**Answer:** ✅ **YES**

Users can:
- Create private gaming groups (invite-only)
- Restrict match invitations to friends
- Control who can message them
- Build private squads for tournaments
